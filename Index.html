ABOUT US

Scientifix AFRIKA is a forward-thinking African initiative committed to science, technology, and innovation across the continent. We empower young minds, bridge knowledge gaps and inspire practical solutions to real-world African challenges through education, digital content and hands-on innovation.



Our mission is to make science simple, relatable and applicable to everyday life while building a new generation of African innovators and problem-solvers.

VISION

To become Africa’s leading platform for scientific innovation, education and youth empowerment.

MISSION

To educate, inspire and equip Africans with practical scientific and technological knowledge that drives innovation, problem-solving and sustainable development.
